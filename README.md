# Detection of Advanced Persistent Threats in Cloud Networks Using Artificial Intelligence

## Research Presentation

A 10-slide research presentation exploring AI-driven methodologies for identifying and mitigating sophisticated APT cyber threats in cloud computing environments.

## Files

1. **Title Slide** - Research title with key statistics

6. **Deep Learning Architectures** - CNN+LSTM, GNN, Transformers/LLM, GANs

## Research Gap Summary

The primary research opportunity identified is the absence of a **cloud-native, explainable, real-time AI framework** that can:
- Correlate multi-stage APT behaviors across distributed cloud environments
- Maintain adversarial robustness against evasion attacks
- Operate in multi-cloud/hybrid settings with privacy-preserving federated learning
